## EDA - SQL Analysis Overview

Within this particular folder, there are a total of 5 SQL files. Each of these contains a particular aspect of portfolio exploratory data analysis. These SQL files are each dedicated to a particular aspect of one of the risks within a portfolio.

- Section 1 deals with overall portfolio health, loan performance, and initial risk distribution by grade, income group, and loan size.
- Section 2 examines how borrowers behave in terms of credit, like utilization stress, the impact of delinquency, and the identification of early warning risk signals.
-  Section 3 examines the interaction of credit grade and income level to identify high-risk borrowers, and the efficacy of risk-based pricing.
-  Section 4 includes an in-depth study of usage patterns such as revolving credit depth, usage frequency, and risky behavior.
-  Section 5 focuses on stability signals such as homeownership, income verification, and job tenure with respect to real-world signals of borrower stability.

The queries can be executed in MS SQL Server, and they will match the output provided in the Excel analysis files corresponding to these sections.
